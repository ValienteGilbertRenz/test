Arnis Club

Player & active club member

Arnis — also known as Eskrima or Kali — is the Philippines' national martial art, built around stick fighting, footwork, and controlled precision. As a player and member of the CSU Arnis Club, I train regularly in strikes, disarms, and sparring, treating it as both a sport and a discipline that carries over into how I approach everything else, including my studies.
